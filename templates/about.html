About Our Project

Our Real-Time Gym Trainer is a college project aimed at enhancing workout routines with AI-driven technology. Key features include:

  • Real-Time Repetition Counting: Tracks reps for consistent exercise.
  • Posture Correction: Provides instant feedback on form.
  • AI-Powered Insights: Offers personalized, real-time guidance.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Accessible for all fitness levels.
  • Collaborative Team Effort: Created by engineering students.
